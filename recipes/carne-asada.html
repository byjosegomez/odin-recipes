Carne Asada

Descrition

Juicy, flavorful carne asada marinated with citrus, garlic, and spices, then grilled to perfection. Perfect for tacos, burritos, or served with rice and beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds flank or skirt steak
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 2 table spoons orange
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Light up your grill and throw it in there
  2. Let's keep it simple like my 1 recipe and cook as you desire
